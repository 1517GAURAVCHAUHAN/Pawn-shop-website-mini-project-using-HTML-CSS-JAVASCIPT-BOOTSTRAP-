What We Accept

We accept a wide range of items, including jewelry, electronics, musical instruments, and more. If you're unsure whether we accept a specific item, give us a call or stop by our store and we'll be happy to help

The Loans Process

When you bring in an item to pawn, we'll assess items value and offer you a loan based on that value. You'll need to provide a valid form of identification and sign a Contact outlining the terms of the loan, including the amout borrowed, interest, you'll revice your item back. If you can't repay the loan, we'll keep the item and sell it to recover our costs.

Our Hours

We're open Monday through Friday from 9am to 6pm, and Saturday from 10am to 4pm.We also offer extended Hours by appointement . Give us a call to schedule a time that works for you.

Fees

Our fees are competitive and transparent. We charge an interest rate of 20% per month on all loans, and a late fee of 10% if you don't make a payment on time. We also charge a storage fee if you don't pick up your item by the agreed-upon date.

Our Inventory

Browse our inventory of items for sale, including pictures and prices. We offer a wide variety of items, from electronics to jewelry to sporting goods.

Manage Your Loan

Use our secure online portal to manage your loan and make payments. You can view your balance, make a payment, and see your payment history all in one place.

About company
